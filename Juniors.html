Developing Our Juniors

"When you get to the end of the tournament, the skill level is pretty much the same. So what makes the difference? Who lasts the longest and who's the strongest mentally." -Sinjin Smith

NCBV Coaches junior teams from the age ranges of 10-22. Juniors receive the full spectrum of training at practice from stretching, conditioning, motor skill development, teamwork, strategy, and tournament preparation

Beyond NCVB

NCBV's goal is to have as many juniors go on to playing sand volleyball at the collegiate level (on scholarship). We will do everything we can to help our players stand out through the recruiting process and move on to next level.
NCAA sanctioned sand volleyball is one of the newly sanctioned sports offered
